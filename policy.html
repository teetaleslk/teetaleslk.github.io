Delivery & Policy

Everything you need to know before you order.

🚚 Islandwide Delivery 🏦 Bank Transfer Payment 🔄 Easy Exchanges 💬 WhatsApp Support

🚚 Delivery

We deliver islandwide across Sri Lanka via registered courier post.
· Colombo & suburbs: usually 2–4 business days after order confirmation
· Outstation areas: usually 4–7 business days
Delivery times can vary slightly during peak seasons — we'll always keep you posted on WhatsApp.

🏦 Payment

TeeTales orders are confirmed and paid via bank transfer. Once you send your order on WhatsApp, we'll confirm stock and share our account details there — nothing is charged automatically, and no card or payment details are ever collected on this website.

🔄 Exchange Policy

Wrong size? Let us know within 3 days of delivery and we'll arrange a size exchange, as long as the tee is unworn, unwashed, and has its tags attached. Since every tee is made to order, we're unable to offer refunds for a simple change of mind — but we'll always work with you to make it right.

✅ Quality Guarantee

Every tee is single-jersey cotton with a vibrant DTF print. If anything arrives damaged or misprinted, message us a photo on WhatsApp and we'll sort it out — free of charge.

💬 Questions?

We reply within 1 hour, 9am–9pm. Reach us any time on WhatsApp.
